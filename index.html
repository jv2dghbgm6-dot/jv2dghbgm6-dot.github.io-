About Narges

Engineering mind.
Healer's heart.

I'm Narges Keyhani — a certified personal trainer and exercise therapist based in Richmond Hill, Ontario. Before moving fully into fitness, I earned a degree in Engineering. That background still shapes everything I do: I approach the human body with the same precision and structural thinking that engineering demands.

But Fitna Club was never meant to be just a training service. It grew into something bigger — a community where women support each other through every season of life, from pregnancy and postpartum to perimenopause, aging, and injury recovery.

Every woman I work with gets a program built entirely around her body, her history, and her goals. No templates. No guesswork.
