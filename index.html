About Me

My professional journey in fitness and health began at 18 years old as a sports instructor in schools. Over time, I expanded my expertise to teach movement at the university level. Driven by a deep commitment to high-quality biomechanics and safe movement quality, I transitioned into working as a certified personal trainer and ultimately specialized as an Exercise Therapist. Today, I combine evidence-based training methods and personalized care to help women restore their core strength, move entirely without pain, and thrive physically through every major lifecycle change.

How I Support Your Journey

Your body experiences unique biological shifts and hormonal stages throughout life. No matter where you stand today, I am with you with customized, strategic physical support.

• Hormonal Transitions & Metabolic Health

Whether you are navigating perimenopause, menopause, or post-menopause, I build target programs focused on shifting body composition, rebuilding bone density, and safely managing stubborn abdominal belly fat.

• Weight Loss Injection Support

If you are using weight loss injections, I structure highly protective resistance training plans and personalized high-protein nutrition tips to ensure you preserve essential lean muscle tissues while losing fat safely.

• Injury Recovery & Movement Limitations

If you are living with physical limitations or returning from an injury, I design precise, biomechanically correct movement protocols to completely remove joint pain, correct posture, and return functional strength to your daily life.
