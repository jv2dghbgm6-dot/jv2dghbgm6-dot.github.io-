About Me

For me, the line between passion and profession doesn’t exist—my work is my passion, and my passion is my work. Every woman who trains with me is unique, which is why I provide deeply individualized care, meticulous monitoring, and a tailored health map designed specifically for her body and goals.

My Journey: From Engineering to Movement Science

Before transitioning fully into the health and fitness industry, I earned my Bachelor’s degree in Engineering from an international university. This unique academic background fundamentally shapes how I look at the human body today.

Instead of relying on generic fitness trends, I view movement through the lens of biomechanics, structural integrity, and functional efficiency. Just like complex mechanical systems, the human body requires precise alignment, balanced forces, and scientific progression to operate at its absolute best without injury.

My Philosophy

At my private boutique fitness studio, I blend evidence-based protocols with therapeutic exercise. Whether your goal is managing chronic joint discomfort, improving post-rehab core strength, or leveling up your functional daily stamina, my approach focuses on customized assessment and scientific programming tailored to your unique anatomy.
