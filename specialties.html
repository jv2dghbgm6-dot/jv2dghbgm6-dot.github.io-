Specialties

Whatever stage you're at, there's a path here.

Menopause & Perimenopause

Hormone-aware programming to manage symptoms, support bone density, preserve muscle mass, and improve energy through the transition.

Pregnancy & Postpartum

Safe, modified movement throughout pregnancy and careful postpartum recovery — including core rehab, diastasis recti, and posture restoration.

Injury & Post-Surgery Rehab

Specialized programming for knee, shoulder, hip, and back concerns — including post-ACL, hip replacement, and orthopedic recovery.

Strength & Functional Fitness

Progressive overload, movement quality, and functional strength training for women at every fitness level.

Healthy Aging (40+)

Balance, mobility, muscle maintenance, and bone health for active aging — with special attention to posture and joint longevity.

Nutrition & Lifestyle Coaching

Protein and fiber targets, meal planning, hydration habits, and behavioral coaching to make healthy eating sustainable.

Your Journey

Wherever you are, there's a program for you.

Life doesn't pause for fitness — so Fitna Club is designed around the seasons of a woman's life.

See How It Works
Preconception

Building your foundation

Start before pregnancy begins. We work on posture, pelvic floor awareness, core activation, and nutrition — so your body enters pregnancy strong and resilient.

Prenatal

Moving safely through pregnancy

Exercise during pregnancy is safe and transformative. We modify programming trimester by trimester. Many clients train up to just days before birth.

Postpartum

Recovering with intention

After a recommended rest period, we carefully rebuild core strength, address diastasis recti, restore posture, and return to full function.

Strength & Aging

For every woman, every stage

Managing menopause, returning from injury, or just ready to build a body that serves you long-term — we meet you exactly where you are.
