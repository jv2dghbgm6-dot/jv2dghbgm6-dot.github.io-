The Process

From first conversation
to lasting strength.

Three Steps

Simple, personal, science-led.

01
Intake & Assessment

We talk through your medical history, current pain points, mobility, goals, and lifestyle — so every decision that follows is grounded in your reality, not assumptions.

02
Your Custom Protocol

I design a tailored movement and nutrition plan using biomechanics principles — addressing your specific imbalances, limitations, and goals safely and progressively.

03
Train, Track & Refine

We work together in studio or online, refining form, monitoring progress, and adjusting your program as your body responds and your goals evolve.

What to Expect

More than just a workout.

Private sessions include postural assessment, movement coaching, form correction, and lifestyle guidance — not just sets and reps. I watch how you move, identify what your body needs, and adjust in real time.

View Pricing & Sessions

Daily Homework

Progress happens between sessions too.

Each client receives take-home movement and nutrition guidance between sessions — short, practical, and designed to fit around your real life.

Morning stretch routines, walking goals, breathing practices, protein targets — small consistent actions that compound over time into lasting change.
